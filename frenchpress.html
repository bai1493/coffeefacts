French Press

This method was invented in 1929, and it is widely considered to be the best and easiest method for a great cup of coffee.


In comparison to other methods, the french press extracts enhanced flavours. The purest flavours of the coffee are conserved through the grounds that are steeped in a coffee pot, then steeped and strained. All of the natural oils, caffeines and antioxidants are preserved with the steeping and straining.


Facts about the process of brewing a french press


  • Grind size: Coarse; to ensure grounds don't seep through the press strainer

  • Brewing Time: 4-5 minutes

  • Flavour profile: Robust and bold, very pure and clean flavour nuances with lots of body
